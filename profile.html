Profile

I’m a 24 years old Delhi-based photographer and PhD researcher in urban studies, with a focus on transport and everyday mobility. My academic work examines how cities function at street level, particularly the relationship between movement, infrastructure, and public space.

Alongside this, my photography documents urban life as it unfolds, observing how people navigate, inhabit, and adapt to the built environment. Influenced by street photography and architectural observation, my work emphasises light, spatial composition, and everyday urban rhythms.

Photography, for me, is both a creative practice and a research tool, allowing me to visually study cities beyond maps, data, and policy frameworks.
